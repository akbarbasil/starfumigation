Phytosanitary Assistance

Phytosanitary assistance refers to the support and services provided to manage and control plant health, typically to prevent or control the spread of plant diseases, pests, and other harmful organisms. This can include various forms of technical support, advice, and interventions designed to protect agricultural and environmental systems.

01. Core Areas of Phytosanitary Support

We provide end-to-end guidance to ensure agricultural cargo complies with international trade standards and plant health rules:

Document Preparation

Complete support with import permits, exporter declarations, and certificate application filings through governmental portals.

Physical Inspections

Pre-shipment inspection of agricultural cargo to ensure freedom from pests, weeds, and pathogens before certificate issuance.

Liaison & Certification

Official liaison with quarantine officers and department representatives (DPPQ&S) for smooth audit and certificate sign-offs.

02. Commodities Requiring Plant Health Clearance

The following agricultural and organic commodities generally require official phytosanitary certifications before cross-border shipping:

  • Fresh Fruits & Vegetables: Highly regulated commodities requiring pest-risk analysis and treatment certification.
  • Grains, Pulses & Seeds: Bulk agricultural foodstuffs vulnerable to store-product pests and weed seed contamination.
  • Timber & Forest Products: Raw logs, lumber, and bamboo materials that could harbor bark beetles or wood-boring larvae.
  • Cut Flowers & Ornamental Plants: Live vegetative materials requiring strict checks for active fungal spores or insect nests.

03. Regulatory Framework

  • Standard Harmonization: Our advisory conforms strictly to the International Plant Protection Convention (IPPC) guidelines.
  • Governmental Operations: We assist in aligning your cargo treatments with the standards set by India's Directorate of Plant Protection, Quarantine & Storage (DPPQ&S), securing worldwide acceptance.
