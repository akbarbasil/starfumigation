Organic Pest Management & Fumigation

Organic fumigation is a method of pest control used in food processing, storage, and transportation that employs natural or non-synthetic substances to fumigate and control pests, without relying on synthetic chemicals. Organic fumigation is especially important in industries or operations where organic certification is required, as it complies with organic farming and food processing standards.

01. Core Goal of Organic Fumigation

Organic fumigation aims to safely eliminate pests such as insects, rodents, and microbes while maintaining food safety and ensuring that the food remains free from harmful chemical residues.

Non-Synthetic Agents

We rely on natural gas compounds and physical methods instead of synthetic chemical pest killers.

Standards Compliant

Fully compliant with international organic food certifications and national agricultural regulations.

Zero Residue

Ensures that premium food commodities maintain their natural taste and aroma with absolutely no chemical traces.

02. Approved Organic Pest Methods

  • Controlled Atmosphere (CA): We substitute oxygen with carbon dioxide (CO₂) or nitrogen to suffocate pests naturally in grain warehouses and storage areas.
  • Thermal Treatment: Raising temperatures in empty storage rooms to eliminate active insect lifecycles without any gas application.
  • Diatomaceous Earth & Natural Dusts: Using organic mechanical barrier powders in crevices to prevent insect nests.
