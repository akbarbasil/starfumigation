Who We Are

About Our Company

STAR FUMIGATION AGENCIES is one of the leading, reliable, and affordable Fumigation and Pest Control companies in India. We operate as a professionally managed organization with highly qualified quarantine experts.

Officially recognized by the Directorate of Plant Protection Quarantine and Storage (DPPQ&S), Ministry of Agriculture, Govt. of India, and certified as an Acceptable Treatment Provider under the Australian Quarantine Inspection Service (AQIS) guidelines.

Compliant with ISPM-15 Standard Guidelines
Regulated Methyl Bromide (MBr) & Phosphine Treatments
Dedicated Customs & Quarantine Clearance Support
Technical Standards

Accreditations & Compliance

NSPM-11 & NSPM-22

Fully compliant with the National Standards for Phytosanitary Measures. We perform certified Methyl Bromide (NSPM-11) and Aluminium Phosphide/Phosphine (NSPM-22) treatments.

AQIS & AFAS Standards

Certified under the Australian Fumigation Accreditation Scheme (AFAS) to issue valid treatment certificates acceptable to Australian border authorities.

ISPM-15 Certification

Authorized to treat and stamp wood packaging materials (WPM), including wooden pallets, crates, and dunnage, ensuring they meet phytosanitary standards for exports.
