Accreditations & Compliance

NSPM-11 & NSPM-22

Fully compliant with the National Standards for Phytosanitary Measures. We perform certified Methyl Bromide (NSPM-11) and Aluminium Phosphide/Phosphine (NSPM-22) treatments.

AQIS & AFAS Standards

Certified under the Australian Fumigation Accreditation Scheme (AFAS) to issue valid treatment certificates acceptable to Australian border authorities.

ISPM-15 Certification

Authorized to treat and stamp wood packaging materials (WPM), including wooden pallets, crates, and dunnage, ensuring they meet phytosanitary standards for exports.
