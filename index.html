Storage Fumigation

Storage fumigation is the process of using fumigants (chemical gases or vapors) to eliminate pests from stored goods, warehouses, or storage facilities.

READ MORE
Food Processing

Fumigation in food processing is a method used to control pests, bacteria, mold, or other contaminants that could affect the quality and safety of food products.

READ MORE
Organic Fumigation

Organic fumigation is a method of pest control used in food processing, storage, and transportation that employs natural or non-synthetic substances to fumigate and control pests, without relying on synthetic chemicals.

READ MORE
ISPM-15 Fumigation

ISPM-15 is a standard developed by the International Plant Protection Convention (IPPC) that governs the treatment of wood packaging materials (WPM) used in international trade.
