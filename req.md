## Website Goal
Redesign the website from a basic layout into a modern, neat, and highly professional digital presence. The site must present STAR FUMIGATION as a premium, government-approved, and AQIS (Australian Quarantine) accredited fumigation and pest control service provider in India.

## Target Audience
- Importers and Exporters (cargo, containers, shipping lines, logistics)
- Food Processing Industries
- Warehouses, Mills, and Storage Facilities
- General commercial facilities requiring pest management

## Key Services
1. **Cargo & Container Fumigation**: Empty and loaded containerized cargo, LCL cargo, ship holds.
2. **Storage & Warehouse Fumigation**: Grains, silos, mills, and large-scale commodities.
3. **Food Processing & Hygiene**: Specialized sanitization and pest management for food machinery.
4. **ISPM-15 Wood Packing Material Treatment**: Pallets, crates, timber treatment with certified marking.
5. **Phytosanitary Certification Assistance**: Documentation and quarantine standards compliance.
6. **Pest Management & Organic Fumigation**: Rodents, termites, and eco-friendly pest controls.

## Redesign Style Guidelines
- **Color Palette**: Sleek white, clean greys, dark blue accents, and brand orange (`#F7941D`) highlight accents.
- **Premium Cards**: Standardized border-radius (`12px` to `20px`), soft drop shadows, and visual badges.
- **Dynamic Elements**: Smooth transitions (`0.3s ease`), hover elevations (`translateY(-5px)`), and clean icon indicators.
- **Visual Placeholders**: High-end CSS/SVG placeholder frames for future team images.

## Content & Image Workflow Context
- **Freelance Content Focus**: This project focuses heavily on rich, industry-accurate content for the fumigation and pest control business.
- **Image Handling**: Real image assets will be provided later by the user's team. For now, use existing assets (like `assets/img/about/about.png`) or clean, modern HTML/CSS placeholder frames.
- **Design Philosophy**: Simple, clean, neat, and highly professional layout rather than over-complicated design.
